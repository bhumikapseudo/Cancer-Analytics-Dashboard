## Project Overview

This project explores national cancer trends across the United States using interactive Tableau dashboards. The analysis focuses on cancer incidence, mortality, survival outcomes, demographic differences, and geographic variation to identify meaningful public health patterns.

The project was developed using publicly available cancer data from the CDC and demonstrates how interactive dashboards can support healthcare research and evidence-based decision making.

---

## Business Problem

Cancer remains one of the leading causes of death worldwide. Public health professionals need intuitive visualizations to understand:

- Trends in cancer incidence over time
- Geographic differences in mortality
- Survival outcomes by cancer type
- Demographic disparities across age, gender, race, and ethnicity
- Lifestyle-related cancer risk factors

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze long-term cancer incidence trends
- Compare mortality rates across U.S. states
- Explore survival rates across different cancer types
- Identify demographic disparities
- Visualize alcohol-associated cancers
- Build an interactive Tableau dashboard for healthcare decision support
